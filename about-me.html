A little about me

With over a decade of experience in customer service, particularly in the grocery retail sector, I've honed my skills in communication, problem-solving, and team management. For 8 years, I held a managerial position, where I led teams to success through effective leadership and a customer-centric approach.

I've always been drawn to the creative possibilities that technology offers. In addition to building gaming computers and being the go-to IT guy for my family, I've spent a significant amount of time exploring Adobe Photoshop as a hobby over the years. I find joy in the process of bringing ideas to life through digital mediums.

In addition to my passion for technology, I have spent the last 2 years cultivating a running and calisthenics routine. I routinely go for a 3+ mile run and follow a body weight workout regiment. It's been incredibly challenging and rewarding. I enjoy learning new ways to build healthy habits and discipline to fuel my growth.

Beyond the hustle of work and workouts, my world revolves around my 10-year-old son, Atlas. We bond over music and video games (of course! what kid doesn't love video games??).
